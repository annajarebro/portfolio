Case: Polestar app in 2025

In this 3 day UX competition, we created the Co-design club. A User Driven Concept that strengthens the Polestar community and generates valuable insights for the company . What we envision is a system where the app and the car are tools that aid the user and Polestar to together shape the future.

Process

We used Figma-Jam to structure the different stages of this design sprint, which was a great way to collaborate remotely and still be able to have an efficient development process. Quite soon we realized that we wanted to focus on an experience and build on "the Polestar feeling". To strengthen our case, we asked people what feeling they think of when they hear Polestar. This eventually led to the question: "Well, what if owners could be a part of creating the Polestar feeling?" . Polestar is known for their speculative precept approach to design, which is a perfect phase to engage users to create value for both the users and Polestar.

Skills & Lesson learned

  • I got a better understanding of how to communicate design concepts.
  • I became more skilled at conducting efficient design processes.
  • I realized that it's possible to generate a large number of ideas in a really short amount of time and that you can create a lot in 3 days.
