Initiated, created, and conducted a workshop in Design Thinking Methodology for team members and managers at the BMLT division at Volvo GTT.

How can I contribute to change? That was the question that sparked the idea of this workshop. I was brought into a team of software developers at Volvo because they wanted to add a UX perspective to their work. My summer internship was limited to 10 weeks. Therefore, I wanted to create something that would help bring a UX perspective to the team in the long run.

If you are not used to brainstorming and other design activities, they can be a bit scary at first. So, to create a safe environment to explore in I created a worksheet to guide and structure the work.

The workshop was highly appreciated, and the material I developed will be reused for other teams. But most importantly, the mindset about design and its use shifted. You can browse the presentation below.
