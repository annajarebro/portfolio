002//003

Reflections

It was a great experience to work together with a start-up and the rapid pace it requires. One of the challenges with creating a product from scratch is prioritization and always evaluating a feature against development time. Working closely with the founder, I got the opportunity to impact strategic design decisions and regularly gather user feedback through interviews and tests.
