WEBSITE CONCEPT

A website concept designed for a small outdoor company.

Anna Jarebro
Webdesign 7,5hp
University of Uppsala

My design approach

The scope of this project was to choose a website, analyze the current UI, and make a redesign. After analyzing the existing site, I formulated the design goal: Create a clear overview of what the company offers in a way that’s inspiring and easy to navigate. One of the main difficulties to overcome was designing with a goal-directed approach without having any direct contact with the company. To solve this problem, I dug around on social media and travel sites to look at reviews and comments. Through this, I started to map out who their customers are and what their goal of visiting the website could be.
