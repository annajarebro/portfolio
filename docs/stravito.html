Stravito - 2022 - Increasing onboarding efficiency

Designing a new feature reducing developer involvement from hundreds to 0 hours when onboarding new customers to Stravito's platform

Context

Knowledge management platform (SaaS)

Stravito is an knowledge management platform used by market research teams at large enterprises like Burberry, Comcast, and Electrolux. At the core, Stravitos platform makes research searchable and centralised. During my time at Stravito I worked with the team responsible for experiences regarding search, upload and AI.

Problem

The process for migrating file systems was not scalable

Onboarding new customers to the platform means migrating anywhere between 300-100k documents. As the company grew, we encountered a scaling bottleneck caused by the manual processes involved in migrating and annotating documents. The process required too much tech involvement, and inefficent annotation workflow for the operations team.
