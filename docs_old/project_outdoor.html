WEBSITE CONCEPT

A website concept designed for a small outdoor company.

Anna Jarebro
Webdesign 7,5hp
University of Uppsala

My design approach

The scope of this project was to choose a website, analyze the current UI, and make a redesign. After analyzing the existing site, I formulated the design goal: Create a clear overview of what the company offers in a way that’s inspiring and easy to navigate. One of the main difficulties to overcome was designing with a goal-directed approach without having any direct contact with the company or their users. To solve this problem, I dug around on social media and travel sites to look at reviews and comments. Through this, I started to map out who their customers are and what their goal of visiting the website could be.

The users

I prefer to use behaivoral personas rather than demographic ones. Personally, I think personas based one behaivour says a lot more about the goals the user might have. For this project I worked toward three main behaivours:

The Tourists: Who wants to book an activity for the entire family that's available on the specific dates they're visiting.

The Bear Grylls: Who wants to dig deep into a specific course and for example book bushcraft basics.

The team builders: Who wishes to make a request for a tailord activity for a larger group of people.

Wireframes

To plan the structure and content of the website I began with sketching low fidelity wireframes. Then moving on to build digital wireframs that evolved until it reached a level of high fidelity.

The fonts

When choosing fonts, the goal was to enhance the outdoor vibe and still make it clean and readable. Therefore I chose a combination of Cubano and Open Sans. Cubano was used for headings as the rounded edges and low crossbars gave the headings a heavy rustic outdoor vibe.

The colors

I wanted to embrace classical outdoor on the website. By using dark shades of green and orange, the colors get a more worn look. Just like that outdoor jacket you inherited from your grandpa that’s still going strong.
